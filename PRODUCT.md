# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, potential collaborators, and other visitors evaluating Samarth's skills and experience. They're scanning quickly to decide whether to reach out, so the site needs to make projects and capability legible fast.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio/resume site that showcases Samarth's projects, skills, and experience, with the goal of generating interest and contact from recruiters or collaborators.

## Brand Personality

Bold, creative, distinctive. Should feel memorable and expressive rather than generic or templated — the kind of site that stands out among other portfolios.

## Anti-references

None specified.

## Design Principles

- Make capability legible at a glance — projects and skills should be scannable in seconds
- Be distinctive over safe — avoid generic template aesthetics
- Show, don't tell — let project work speak louder than self-description
- Every page should make the case for getting in touch

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No specific requirements beyond standard good practice.
